Mood Cloud

The purpose behind the Mood Cloud project is to raise student awareness about emotional intelligence and to provide a safe space for students to reach out to trusted teachers or leaders in times of emotional crisis or despair. Students often suffer from emotional instability making it difficult to identify emotional patterns over time. Mood Cloud helps.

Built in January 2018. Co-collaborators: Eddie Po, Nathan Garrett, and Quang Nguyen

Sitter Finder

Sitter Finder's mission is to make child care more affordable and accessible to parents. The technology we're building simplifies the process of finding a baby sitter for a special occasion or if needed on a regular schedule. The technology will also allow parents to search for certified sitters based on location.

Built in November 2017. Co-collaborator: Nathan Garrett

Hola Mundo

Hola Mundo is a Spanish language learning app built with React on the front-end and MondoDB on the back-end. The app allows uses Redux for state management and JWT for authentication. MongoDB being a non-relational database allows for flexible changes to the back-end schema as front-end features are developed.
